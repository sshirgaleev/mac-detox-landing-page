Common Questions

Everything you need to know about MacDetox and how it works.

01. Why is my Mac still full even after deleting files?

Because macOS doesn't remove cached data, app leftovers, logs, Xcode junk, or temporary system files. Those can stack into tens of gigabytes — and Finder doesn't show them.

02. How is MacDetox different from the built-in macOS storage cleanup?

macOS only clears a small portion of temporary files. MacDetox scans deep developer caches, browser junk, app leftovers, GPU logs, Homebrew trash, and more — the things Apple never touches.

03. Can MacDetox remove apps completely with all leftovers?

Yes. The Full Uninstall feature removes the app + hidden support files, caches, launch agents, preferences, and daemon processes. No more "uninstalled" apps taking 2GB in ~/Library.

04. Will MacDetox actually make my Mac faster — or just free space?

Both. Freeing disk space improves SSD performance, but we also remove background services, launch agents, and bloated caches that slow the system down — especially on older Macs.
